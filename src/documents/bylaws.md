## Our By Laws

### Membership

#### Eligibility

- Workers are eligible for membership in the cooperative after a year of active
  service.
- Consumers are eligible for membership upon network service contract.
- Community members eligible for membership must be sponsored by an existing
  member. Community members are not eligible for votes.

#### Shares

- Worker shares cost $100.
- Consumer shares cost $100.
- Community members shares are free.

#### Termination

- Worker owner's membership is terminated with employment.
- Consumer owner's membership is terminated with service contract.
- Community member membership is terminated after 6 months of inactivity.

#### Member meetings

- All members meet together quarterly.
- Worker members meet monthly

### Board of Directors

- **President**: Ensures consensus is gathered, makes sure concerns are heard.
- **Secretary**: Makes sure notes are taken.
- **Treasurer**: Reviews financial documents.

### Financials and Profits

- Surplus can be earmarked for capital expenditure projects by a vote of the
  general membership.
- Any additional funds are redistributed to workers and consumers.
- Should the cooperative disolve, all hardware will be donated to a cooperative.

### Administration

- Amendments require 70% majority, though we strive for consensus.
